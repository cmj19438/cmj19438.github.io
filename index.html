University of Georgia's Top 3 2017 Freshman Recruits

#1.) 5-star S Richard LeCounte III (Riceboro, GA)

At 5-11 and 185 lbs Richard Lecounte III is the number 1 recruit for UGA coming in the fall of 2017. He will be a beast on the defensive end with a high football IQ, but not is also a dual threat with the ability to return the football. Once he is able to bulk up and adjust to the collegiate football level, the sky is the limit for the young 5-star recruit.

#2.) 4-star QB Jake Fromm (Warner Robins, GA)

Jake Fromm is a local Georgia boy that has always dreamed to play for the Bulldogs at Sandford Field. He is a prostyle quaterback that has a hammer for an arm with extreme accuracy. He threw 1062 yards and 108 touchdowns in highschool with only 20 interceptions. He has a lot of work to do before he gets that starting spot over Eason.

#3.) 4-star WR Jeremiah Holloman (Covington, GA)

Only playing for 2 years at his local high school, Holloman totaled 1083 yards and 10 touchdowns. After a poor overall display from the wide recievers last year, Holloman will add strength to the team and another weapon Eason can target. At 6-2 and 195 lbs, he should be an easy target to see in the field and be able to blow past small safties and linebackers once he gets adjusted to a higher compitetion of football.
